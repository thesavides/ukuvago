What's a SAFE Note?

Simple Agreement for Future Equity

SAFE is an investment instrument developed by Y Combinator. It's not a loan‚Äîit's a contractual right to future equity when a priced round occurs.

Faster

No immediate valuation negotiation.

Founder-Friendly

No interest/repayment. Minimal dilution until priced round.

Investor-Friendly

Valuation cap protection & discount on future rounds.

Key Terms:

  • Valuation Cap: Max company valuation at conversion.
  • Discount Rate: Percentage discount on future price (10-20%).
  • Post-Money: Calculated after investment.
